Freshman checklist

Follow these steps in order to stay ahead during your first quarter.

Before move-in

  • Accept offer and confirm enrollment in MyStudentCenter
  • Complete health forms and vaccinations
  • Watch orientation videos and sign up for TritonLink

Week 0

  • Attend New Student Orientation and college welcome events
  • Meet your roommate and start your fall schedule
  • Set up campus ID, email, and key apps

First month

  • Meet your academic advisor and declare your major interests
  • Explore tutoring, campus recreation, and student clubs
  • Build a weekly schedule and establish a study routine

Quick FAQ

Short answers for the questions most new Tritons ask first.

Use MyStudentCenter to enroll in classes. Your college advising team can help confirm GE and major requirements.

Meet your advisor during the first two weeks of the quarter. Advising appointments often fill quickly, so book early.

CAPS and campus wellness services are available for every student. Study centers and peer mentors also offer academic support.
